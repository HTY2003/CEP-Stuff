the grounds that witnesses had been influenced by his immunized testimony). one of the charges against north was that he had received a $16,000 home security system paid for out of the proceeds of the iran-contra affair and had forgeddocuments to cover his receipt of an illegal gratuity. north admitted that he knew the security system was a gift but maintained he never inquired about who had paid family.the terrorist north mentioned in his testimony was not osama bin laden, however. to the extent that bin laden was known to the western world in 1987, it was not as a terrorist but as one of the u.s.-backed freedom fighters participating in the war against the soviet occupation of afghanistan. osama bin ladens hatred of the u.s. and conversion to terrorist status is not believed to have come
